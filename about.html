About Us




Nowadays , camping is becoming one of the most growing industry in the world. In fact, it’s raising day after day to reach the top of community’s standings.
In addition, this industry is neglected and completely forgotten in Tunisia .
Also, campers don’t find the support and framing they need to practice their passion as they wish.
And all of that leads to big difficulties that encounter campers and discourage them especially beginners. As we all know , there is no interface in Tunisia that reunites all the camping community and facilitate the task for them ,that’s why we decided to make a permanent solution with the hope of making a difference and help our society ❤❤
The camping culture is not widespread in the Arab world, but when you know what camping can offer you healthily, mentally and emotionally, you will want to try it, because of the important benefits that camping provides.
Don't let time steal you, call your friends, escape the cityscape, plan a camping night, an outdoor party or even a walk, this will be like recharging your body and mind's battery, so don't deprive yourself of it.
